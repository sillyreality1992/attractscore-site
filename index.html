FAQ

Do you store my photos or videos?

No. You share a link to your photo or video (for example Google Drive, Instagram, iCloud). You keep your media where it is — no direct uploads to our servers.

Why a range instead of one exact score?

Attraction is subjective. A range is more honest and reduces false precision.

What’s the difference between Global and Regional?

Global reflects overall ratings from people worldwide. Regional reflects ratings relative to your selected region.

Is this app on iOS?

Not yet. Right now it’s Android only. iOS is planned for later.
