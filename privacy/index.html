Privacy Policy – AttractionScore

AttractionScore respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the AttractionScore mobile application.

Information we collect

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Gender
  • Country and state (approximate location)
  • User ID (Firebase Authentication)
  • Uploaded images (provided by users)
  • Purchase history (handled securely via Google Play Billing)
  • App interaction data
  • Device or other identifiers

How we use your information

  • To create and manage user accounts
  • To provide attraction rating services
  • To process purchases and services
  • To improve app performance and user experience
  • To comply with legal obligations

Data storage and security

Data is securely transmitted using encryption in transit. We use trusted third-party services such as Firebase and Google Play Billing.

Data sharing

We do not sell or share personal data with third parties for advertising purposes. Data is only shared with service providers required to operate the app.

Data retention

User data is retained only as long as necessary to provide services or comply with legal requirements.

Account and data deletion

Users may request deletion of their account and associated data by emailing: support@attractionscore.com
Subject: Delete My AttractionScore Account

Account and associated data are deleted within 7–14 days after verification. Purchase records may be retained as required by law.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: support@attractionscore.com
